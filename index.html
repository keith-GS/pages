What I'm Building

Private capital runs on quarterly reports and spreadsheets. That means the people managing billions of dollars in alternatives - venture, PE, credit, real assets - are making decisions on data that's 30 to 90 days stale. GoodStream fixes that.

We deploy specialized AI extraction agents that pull position-level data from the messy, unstructured documents that funds actually produce - capital call notices, distribution notices, NAV statements, K-1s. We normalize it, reconcile it, and deliver it in real time.

The thesis is simple: the data already exists, it's just trapped in PDFs. We get it out.
